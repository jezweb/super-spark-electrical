Our Services

We provide a comprehensive range of electrical services for homeowners in Western Sydney, with a focus on quality workmanship and customer satisfaction.

Switchboard Upgrades & Repairs

Modern, safe switchboards to protect your home and family from electrical hazards.

Learn More

Your switchboard is the heart of your home's electrical system. Our switchboard services include:

  • Safety switch installations
  • Fuse box to circuit breaker upgrades
  • Switchboard relocations
  • Surge protection installation
  • Complete switchboard replacements

We use only the highest quality materials and ensure all work complies with Australian electrical standards.

Ceiling Fan Installation

Energy-efficient cooling solutions for year-round comfort in your home.

Learn More

Ceiling fans are a cost-effective way to improve comfort and reduce energy bills. Our ceiling fan services include:

  • New ceiling fan installations
  • Replacement of old or noisy fans
  • Installation of remote-controlled fans
  • Fan with light combination installations
  • Outdoor ceiling fan installations

We can install your purchased fan or help you select the perfect model for your space.

Home Rewiring

Complete or partial rewiring to ensure your home's electrical system is safe and up to code.

Learn More

Older homes often have outdated wiring that can pose serious safety risks. Our rewiring services include:

  • Full house rewiring
  • Partial rewiring for renovations
  • Replacement of old, damaged, or dangerous wiring
  • Upgrading from old rubber or cloth wiring
  • Installation of additional circuits for modern appliances

We minimise disruption to your home during the rewiring process and ensure all work meets current electrical codes.

Power Points & Lighting

Additional power points and modern lighting solutions for your home.

Learn More

Modern homes need adequate power points and efficient lighting. Our services include:

  • Installation of additional power points
  • USB power point upgrades
  • Outdoor power point installation
  • LED lighting upgrades
  • Sensor lighting installation
  • Pendant light installation

We can help you plan the optimal placement for new power points and lighting to enhance your home's functionality.

Electrical Safety Inspections

Comprehensive safety checks to identify and address potential electrical hazards.

Learn More

Regular electrical safety inspections are essential for preventing electrical fires and ensuring your family's safety. Our inspection services include:

  • Pre-purchase home inspections
  • Rental property safety checks
  • Smoke alarm compliance checks
  • Electrical fault finding
  • Safety switch testing

We provide detailed reports and recommendations to address any safety concerns identified during the inspection.

Emergency Electrical Services

Rapid response for electrical emergencies to keep your family safe.

Learn More

Electrical emergencies require immediate attention. Our emergency services include:

  • Power outage troubleshooting
  • Circuit breaker tripping issues
  • Burning smell from outlets or switches
  • Exposed or damaged wiring
  • Storm damage repairs

Our emergency team is available to quickly address dangerous electrical situations and restore safety to your home.
